FAQ

Good things to know.

What do you sell at Arvind Canteen? +

We sell traditional Indian sweets (mithai), savory snacks like samosa and golgappa, bakery items, and custom birthday cakes. All vegetarian.

Are the sweets made fresh? +

Yes — sweets and snacks are made fresh daily. Customers consistently praise the freshness and authentic taste.

Do you make custom birthday cakes? +

Yes, we make custom celebration cakes. Come in or call us to place your order in advance.

Where exactly is Arvind Canteen? +

We're on Main Road, near UCO Bank, in front of Patanjali, Barharwa, Jharkhand 816101.

What are your opening hours? +

We are open daily and close at 10 pm. Come in anytime for fresh sweets, snacks, or a quick bite.

Do you offer takeaway? +

Yes — takeaway and dine-in both available. We also offer delivery and catering for events.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We currently accept cash only.
